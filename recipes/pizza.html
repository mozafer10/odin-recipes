Pizza

Description

This is an easy recipe to make pizza

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough with roller
  2. Add tomato sauce with spoon
  3. Sprinkle cheese evenly
  4. Put uncooked pizza in oven for 10 mins
  5. Take out pizza and top with parsley
  6. Enjoy!
